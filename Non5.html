Tanduri chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp tandoori masala
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, tandoori masala, ginger-garlic paste, and lemon juice.
  2. Let it sit for at least 1-2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  4. Place the marinated chicken on a baking tray.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes until cooked and slightly charred.
