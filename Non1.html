Butter chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes,1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste,1 tsp red chili powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric powder Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp butter 1 large onion, finely chopped 2 tomatoes, pureed, 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala, 1 tsp coriander powder, 1 tsp cumin powder, Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate: Mix chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook Chicken: Cook in butter until done. Set aside.
  3. Make Gravy: Saut√© onions in butter, add tomato puree, and spices. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Combine: Add chicken and cream to gravy. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve: Garnish with coriander and serve with naan or rice.
